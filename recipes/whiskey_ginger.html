Whiskey Ginger

Description

This basic but delicious cocktail is a mix of irish whiskey and ginger ale.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces irish whiskey, to taste
  • 5 ounces ginger ale
  • 1 lime wedge

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Pour the whiskey into a highball glass filled with ice.
  3. Fill with ginger ale.
  4. Squeeze a lime wedge over the drink and drop it in.
  5. Stir.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
