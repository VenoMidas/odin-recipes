Sazerac

Description

The Sazerac is a close cousin to the classic Old Fashioned. It was crowned the offical cocktail of New Orleans in 2008.

Ingredients

  • Absinthe, to rinse
  • 2 ounces rye whiskey, to taste
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 3 dashes Peychaud's Bitters
  • lemon twist for garnsih

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Chill an old-fashined glass by filling it with ice. Let it sit while you prepare the rest of the drink.
  3. In a mixing glass, soak the sugar cube with the bitters and muddle to crush the cube.
  4. Add the rye whiskey and stir.
  5. Discard the ice in the chilled glass. Rinse it with absinthe: Pour a small amount into the glass, swirl it around, then discard the absinthe. You can also add the extra absinthe to your drink if preferred.
  6. Pour the whiskey mixture into the absinthe-rinsed glass.
  7. Gently squeeze the lemon twist over the drink. Traditionalists typically discard it and rarely drop it into the glass; lay it on the rim as a garnish if you like. Serve and enjoy.
